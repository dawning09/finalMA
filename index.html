My favorite person in the entire world is my dog, Kulfi. She is a Shih Tzu who was named Margret before we got her. Shih tzus were originally from Tibet where they lived in the Buddhist monasteries before they were gifted to the emperors of China. When in China, they were royal lap dogs and if you were caught with one outside the palace, you were exhausted. Thankfully that is a rule that no long exists but it dwindled the breed down to 14 dogs.
